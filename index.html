WELCOME TO VILLA MIMOZA

Wyatt’s Hotel is located at 109 South 3rd Street, Tombstone AZ, located on the East End of the Schieffelin Historic District and world famous Allen Street. Situated across the street from the world famous O.K. Corral and the famous 1881 gun fight site and also the 1882 Cochise County Courthouse; the second Arizona State Park, where 7 hangings took place. Wyatt’s Hotel is perfectly positioned for a short walk to all the saloons, restaurants, numerous shops and mining tours! Adjacent to Wyatt’s Hotel & Coffee House is the Saloon Theater which hosts re-enactments of true historical gun fights that are a part of Tombstone’s once wild and infamous past. Wyatt’s Hotel offers a unique Victorian Parlor area upon the entrance to the Suites where hotel guests can sit and enjoy free tea and coffee on our beautiful antique furniture or enjoy in your suite. Free Wi Fi is accessible in all areas of Wyatt’s Hotel and Coffee House.
